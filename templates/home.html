Online Examination For Anyone

This online examination platform is a software solution that allows any institute or industry to plan, organize and administer examinations through the internet/intranet and Local Area Network(LAN) environments. In an online exam, a teacher or examiner creates various kinds of questions(Multiple Choice Questions[MCQ] as well as Subjective Type Questions, The user is just need to be loggedin, in order to participate in the examination. The result of MCQ type Questions will be displayed on the screen itself after completion of exam & the Subjective type will be save on teacher's/Examiner's side. The Examiner must have to add the subject name & time duration for the exam. After all these things, you are ready to enjoy the online examination.
